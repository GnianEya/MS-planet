Who We Are

A Story About Us

MS-Planet's mission is to inspire and develop people to achieve their goals in STEM — one person, one question, and one small commitment to learning at a time.

We enable great teachers to illuminate the soul of math, science, and engineering through bite-sized, interactive learning experiences. Our courses explore the laws that shape our world, elevating math and science from something to be feared to a delightful experience of guided discovery.

How to get started

Our courses

01

Apply to the program

Apply for an Education account by filling out our signup form. We’ll also ask you to complete a survey to assess your needs.

02

Create classes and assign content

Once verified and accepted, educators can choose up to 6 courses and unlimited Daily Challenges that each of their classes will have access to.

03

Invite your students

Invite your students and get started! Feel free to email educators@msplanet.org with any questions or feedback.
